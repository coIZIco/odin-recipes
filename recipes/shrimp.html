Grilled Teriyaki Shrimp and Pineapple Skewers

Description

These perfect for summer shrimp and pineapple skewers are sweet and savory. They make for the perfect dish at dinner parties

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup water
  • 2 tbs soy sauce
  • 2 tbs brown sugar
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1 tsp grated fresh garlic
  • 1 tsp grated fresh ginger
  • 1 lb jumbo shrimp
  • 1/2 fresh pineapple, cored and cut into 1 1/2 inch pieces
  • minced fresh cilantro (optional)
  • toasted sesame seeds (optional)

Steps

  1. Combine water, soy sauce, brown sugar, honey, garlic, and ginger in a small sauce pan and bring to a boil over medium-high heat. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until sauce has reduced and thickened slightly, 8-10 minutes.
  2. Preheat outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil grate. Thread shrimp and pineapple alternately onto skewers.
  3. Arrange skewers on the hot grate. Grill 2-3 minutes per side, or until shrimp is opaque and cooked through. Turn grill to low heat and brush saauce on both sides of the skewers. Transfer to a serving plate and sprinkle with cilantro and sesame seeds.
